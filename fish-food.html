At J and J Aquafarms, 32 years of demanding field use have resulted in the best choice for your prized pond or aquarium fish: Pescapro!

Pescapro fish foods are professional grade and formulated to meet the precise demands of serious fish culturists. They contain the essential micronutrients and protein quality necessary for healthy growth and breeding in recirculating or flow-through aquatic environments.

The web has enabled unprecedented availability of fresh, high-end Pro- quality aquatic rations to the individual fish grower. Because J and J Aquafarms turns over tons of food per month, Pescapro is guaranteed fresh with a full compliment of active vitamins, unlike other foods with degraded nutrients that have been shelf-stored for months.

Featuring our specially formulated J&J Aquafarms medicated food with an all natural binder that is stable for 15 minutes!
