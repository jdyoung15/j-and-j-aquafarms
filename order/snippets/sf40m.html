Antibiotic Food

2.6 grams nitrofurazone per kg for battling bacterial infections from Aeromonas and Columnaris, the cause of most fish bacterial infections. Antibiotic is bound to the pellet with a natural, edible, binder that lasts for at least 20 minutes in water, enough time for your fish to ingest and get medicated. Save those prized fish! For ornamental fish only. Proper care of fish is preferable to use of antibiotics, which should be used in emergencies as a last resort.
