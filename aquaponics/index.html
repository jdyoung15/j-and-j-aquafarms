Aquaponics is the integration of two sciences; Aquaculture and Hydroponics. Interest in aquaponics is at a high now that healthy, suatainable fish are becoming a preferred component of our diets.

From the urban farmer to the Pacific Islander, aquaponics answers the worldwide questions of

  • How can we continue to supply fish to a growing population?
  • How can we mitigate the loss of wild fish stocks?
  • Is there a safer food alternative for my family?
  • Can I grow vegetables and protein with limited space, water,and funds?
  • What can I do to promote a healthy, sustainable lifestyle?

You can accomplish all this with an aquaponic system scaled to your space and needs. If you have kept even a goldfish in a bowl, you already have the inherent skills to be an aquaponic farmer!
