Caruthers High School

The Agriculture Technology Class is building an Aquaponic system and outdoor earthen pond as a teaching laboratory.

J and J Aquafarms designed the compact three-tank system using available materials with minimal cost. Bluegill Sunfish and lettuce were initially stocked in December 2011.
