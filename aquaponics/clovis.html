Clovis Water Department Demonstration Aquaponics

The city of Clovis, CA gets much of its water from the cold Sierra Nevada snowmelt. It is run through a state-of-the-art treatment plant to make sure it is at its absolute cleanest.

Water Production Manager Leon Penney wants to show folks even more uses for water. In this geodesic greenhouse, he has built an aquaponics system that teaches the synergism of aquaculture and hydroponics.
