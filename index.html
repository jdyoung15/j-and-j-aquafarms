Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day.
Teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime.

This wise saying has rung true for millennia. We are now facing a world of diminishing fish numbers and an increasing human population that demands more fish in its diet. Experts claim that, at the current rate, we will deplete the wild fish stocks by 2050.

The solution to this problem is Aquaculture. We may now add to the venerable words of wisdom:

Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day.
Teach a man to fish and you feed him until the fish are gone.
Show a man how to grow fish and he will eat for a lifetime.

J and J Aquafarms has supplied the Aquaculture, Research, and Recreational communities for over 29 years. Our mission is equipping folks with the resources needed to supply healthy fish to local communities, using appropriate technologies.
